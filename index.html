BEFORE YOU BOOK

What You'll Need to Rent

A little preparation makes pick-up day quick. Here's everything to have ready before your confirmation call.

  • Valid driver's license, held for at least 1 year
  • One valid government-issued ID
  • Credit or debit card for the security deposit
  • Minimum age of 21 (under-25 drivers may incur a young-driver fee)
  • Additional drivers must present their own valid license

Good to Know

  • Return the car with a full tank to skip refueling fees.
  • Walk around the vehicle with our staff before you drive off.
  • Save your confirmation message — you'll need it at pick-up.
  • Let us know early if you need a child seat or GPS unit.
